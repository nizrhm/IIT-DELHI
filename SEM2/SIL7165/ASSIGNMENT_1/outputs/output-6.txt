Deciphered Plaintext: the industrial revolution was the transition to new manufacturing processes in great britain, continental europe, and the united states, in the period from about seventeen sixty to sometime between eighteen twenty and eighteen forty. this transition included going from hand production methods to machines, new chemical manufacturing and iron production processes, the increasing use of steam power and water power, the development of machine tools and the rise of the mechanized factory system.
